Chris' Steamed Buns
-------------------

Servings: 24

Ingredients:
2 tablespoons white sugar
1/4 cup water
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Directions:
1. Mix together yeast, 1 teaspoon sugar, 1/4 cup flour, and 1/4 cup warm water. Allow to stand for 30 minutes.
2. Mix in 1/2 cup warm water, flour, salt, 2 tablespoons sugar, and vegetable oil.
3. Knead until dough surface is smooth and elastic. Roll over in a greased bowl, and let stand until triple in size, about 2 1/2 to 3 hours.
4. Punch down dough, and spread out on a floured board.
6. Divide dough into 2 parts, and place the piece you are not working with in a covered bowl. Divide each half into 12 parts.
8. Bring water to a boil in wok, and reduce heat to medium; the water should still be boiling.
9. Place steam-plate on a small wire rack in the middle of the wok.
11. Cover wok with lid and steam buns over boiling water for 15 minutes.
13. Continue steaming batches of buns until all are cooked.
